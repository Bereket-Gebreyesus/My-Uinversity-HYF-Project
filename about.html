About Mekelle Institute of Technology

Mekelle Institute of Technology (MIT) is an educational institute created in Mekelle, the capital of the Tigray Region of Ethiopia,
in 2002,aiming to teach and do research in engineering, science and technology. In 2020, MIT integrated with Mekelle University.
MIT graduated its first batch of 142 students on July 7, 2007. In 2007, 560 students studied in the MIT Faculty of Engineering.
